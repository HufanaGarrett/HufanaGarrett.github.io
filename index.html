About

I am currently a Forestry student at the University of Kentucky. My hobbies include hiking, rock climbing, bicycling and hunting for snakes! Much of my work is focused on, but not limited to, terrain and navigation. I created this page to display my work and provide resources to other map makers and adventurers.

Mission

I want this to be a collection of maps that focus on navigation through National Parks and public land. I have always enjoyed exploring through the woods in search of fossils, wildlife and adventure. I want to be able to provide maps to areas where people such as myself can explore and find their own entertainment.
